About Us

Ravintolalaiva m/s Bruno is a family‑owned restaurant that combines traditional Finnish cuisine with fresh sea produce. We have established our restaurant on Hahdenniementie, Raisio, and aim to provide our customers with an unforgettable culinary experience on every visit.

We offer both intimate dinner evenings and larger celebrations, and our kitchen uses only the finest ingredients from local suppliers.
